	name "Gathering Specimens: Alexandria"

	to offer
		or
			"Transport scientists: done" > 5
			has "Deep: Project Hawking: done"
	to complete
		never
	on offer
		conversation
			`A group of scientists approach you. "Captain <first> <last>? We've been told that you're a reliable captain who travels beyond The Deep. If that's true, would be interested in helping us with our research?"`
			choice
				`	"What sort of help do you need?"`
			`	"Nothing too hard. Just keep an eye out for any interstellar life forms. If you find one, bring us back a tissue sample. We'll make sure you are compensated."`
				accept

mission "Gathering Specimens: Conservatory Conclusion"
	landing
	invisible
	source
		government "Free Worlds" "Republic" "Syndicate" "Neutral" "Independent" "Pirate"
		not attributes "uninhabited"
	destination Winter
	to offer
		has "event: Gathering Specimens: Conservatory Research"
	on offer
		dialog `You receive a message from the Free Worlds Conservatory administrators: "Your contributions have lead to a research break through! Visit the Conservatory on <destination> to learn more."`
	on complete
		"salary: Free Worlds Conservatory" = 3000
		conversation
			scene "scene/scene-specimens"
			`The administrators thank you for helping with the research. And now they present the results of that work: a cure for Chronic Hyperspace Traveler's Disorder.`
			choice
				`	"I've never heard of that."`
